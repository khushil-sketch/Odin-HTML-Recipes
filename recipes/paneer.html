Chilli Paneer Recipe

What is Chilli Paneer?

Chilli paneer is a popular Indo-Chinese starter made by seasoning fried Indian cottage cheese aka paneer in chilli sauce.

It is a delicious appetizer where. corn flour coated paneer are crisp fried first and then tossed in a flavorful indo Chinese freshly made chilli sauce along with stir fry onions, capsicum and spring onions.

Ingredients

  • ½ kg paneer cut into medium sized cubes
  • 3 tablespoons corn flour
  • 3 tablespoons gram flour
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 ½ tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon chilli powder
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 ½ tablespoon garlic paste
  • 4 green chillies chopped
  • 2 white onions cut into medium sized cubes
  • 3 capsicums cut into medium sized cubes
  • 3 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • ½ cup red chilli sauce
  • ¼ cup tomato ketchup
  • 1 bunch chopped coriander
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Mix cornflour and water in a bowl and keep aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok.
  3. Add spring onion white, ginger and garlic and fry for a minute on high heat.
  4. Add onion, green chilli and capsicum and fry for another minute.
  5. Add onions and capsicum and fry for 2-3 minutes on high heat.
  6. Add the cornflour slurry and cook until the sauce thickens.
  7. Now add red chilli paste, soy sauce, vinegar, salt, sugar and black pepper powder and cook for a minute.
  8. Switch off the heat and let the sauce cool down a bit.
  9. After 5 minutes, add the fried paneer in the sauce and toss well to coat them with the sauce.
  10. Garnish with spring onion greens and sesame seeds.
  11. SUAVE
